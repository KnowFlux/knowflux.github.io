Purity

In all of his 13 years, this day would be the most chaotic of C142’s life - which was no exaggeration. In the Pure Cities, everything was the same.

The similarities began with clothing. Each citizen was to wear the same crisp white shirt with a starched collar and the same stiff white pants, which made all tasks from sitting to standing an issue.

Their schedule, too, was identical. Each day, citizens under the age of 20 would wake up at 0700 hours and depart for the Pured Institute at 1000 hours. Arrival was at 1030 hours. In the next 5 minutes, they were to find their assigned seats within the Institute, and remain straight-backed and unmoving till 1500 hours. During this time, they were lectured on Purity and Impurity and their importance.

They would then stand as one promptly, then line up by the door single file. They should arrive at home by 1630 hours, and leave for the Bureau of Operational Timing at 1745 hours, and be there by 1800 hours. There, they would receive information and schedule changes (if there were any changes).

They would then swear the Pledge of Unblemished Dawn at 1900 hours and would sleep in their respective dwellings at 2000 hours.

As a result, life for C142 had been the same since he was 7, and he was quite content - until the disastrous events of today.

The day had begun as normal, with him waking up at 0700 hours and having his daily nutrient pills and some water before putting on the uniform. Unknown to him - and not for long - this was as normal as his day was to be. As he exited his dwelling - a medium-sized, rather boxy, and whitewashed one - a stark white Itineror coupled with deep blue stripes parked, humming with energy, right outside C145’s door - a neighbor three dwellings down the 100’s section of Block C. Instant recognition flashed into his mind: this was a Purist Itineror.
