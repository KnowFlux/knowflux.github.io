The Pinnacle of Reality: Book 1

For thirteen quiet years, C142 has lived within the Pured Cities - sterile fortresses built on the law of Purity, where anything Impure is hunted down and erased. Order is enforced. Safety is manufactured. Freedom? An illusion. But in one terrible day, the system shatters, and the very thing the Pured Cities abhorr erupts into catastrophic reality. Instantly, C142's world fractures. From the wreckage comes a forbidden call - dangerous, magentic, and utterly opposed to the Purity that shaped him. It shouldn't exist. He shouldn't want it. But it pulls at him with the force of truth. Caught between the anvil of mindlessness and awakening, truth and falsehood, call and duty, his choices have ramifications beyond what he ever imagined, destabilizing a world already cracking under the weight of its lies. But throughout the chaos, one truth is clear: what's at stake is nothing less than reality itself.
