Rhythm of the Red River

A Triplet

Sticks and stones and mournful moans

Staring skulls and broken bones

Three killed kings and their three thrones

Triplets

A triplet is a stanza of three lines in poetry. They may be rhymed or unrhymed. In many cases, triplets follow an AAA rhyme scheme where all three lines rhyme together. This triplet is a dark, brooding one which tries to capture the mood of death.
