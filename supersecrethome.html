The Pinnacle of Reality: Book 1

C142 has lived a peaceful and ordinary life for the past 13 years in the Pured Cities - cities which have been cleansed for anything Impure and abnormal. But when the very thing that the Pured Cites abhorr lead to catastrophic events, the ramifications are terrible. Suddenly, C142's life is reversed - everything that he knows is destroyed. What's more, something else is calling him. A call that goes against the very foundations of the Pured Cities - the only thing he has left - but at the same time he cannot resist its pull. The revolution it invites him to is the only one which feels truly right - but so wrong at the same time. So against everything he was brought up with. Caught between the anvil of contrasting emotions, callings, and duty, tremendous decisions lead to unimaginable consequences. The worst part? Reality itself is at stake...
