Purity

The Purists were a law enforcement agency, whose goal was to eliminate anomalies - anything which contributed to Impurity. Their aim was a noble one - everything felt so wrong, so Impure. As if reality was cracked glass that might give way any moment. If the Purists would solve this, he would support them.

But why choose C145 as their target? C142 had always disliked C145. C145 was apparently from another city and was always so cheery. He always waved if he saw someone coming across the street, unknowingly breaking Article 18, section B, subsection I, line 4, “Civilians must not interact unless in an emergency."

Perhaps this constant law-breaking was the cause.

C145 exited his dwelling slowly, his hands in the air. But a grinding, wrenching sound shattered the tense silence and the ground cracked and shattered, swallowing Purists and their Itinerors. Somehow C145 was fine - he’d stumbled a bit, but was somehow on the other side of the gap, continuing toward the Purists with his hands raised.

Shock and horror pervaded C142’s mind, and his panic, he dashed towards his dwelling, his mind reeling. But he was forced to stop, the ground gaping in front of him. He’d have to take a longer route, around the wide crack. He turned and continued running, but the ground shook and the air was filled with dust and dirt. He fell, grinding against the rough, rocky ground. He ignored the pain and attempted to stand, but he was unable. Instead, he managed to turn his head toward the now screaming Purists and the calm C145, not out of balance at all - and then the scene was blurred by a haze of dust and smoke.

He was aware of a thrumming sound, and from the blanket of dust covering C145, a large white shape emerged, spinning through the air towards him. In terror he scrambled away, scraping against jagged metal and rock, but he continued grimly. He managed his feet and half ran, half jumped, narrowly avoiding the flying object, but his avoidance was for nothing. The Itineror - for he now recognized it as the white shape - violently exploded, shards of metal flying like deadly spears in all directions. The shock waves flung him onto the street, and he felt something shear across his stomach. A warm liquid coated the area, and through his dim vision, the front of his shirt appeared red.
